# Documentation Style Guide (Sample)

## Purpose
This guide ensures consistency, clarity, and readability across technical documentation.

---

## Writing Principles

- Use clear and concise language  
- Avoid unnecessary repetition  
- Prefer short sentences over long, complex ones  
- Use active voice where possible  

---

## Formatting

- Use headings to structure content logically  
- Use bullet points for lists  
- Maintain consistent terminology throughout the document  

---

## Technical Content

- Do not alter technical meaning while editing  
- Clarify ambiguous statements by simplifying language  
- Validate consistency across related sections  

---

## Error Messages

- Keep messages short and actionable  
- Avoid vague wording  

---

## Notes
This is a sample guide demonstrating approach to documentation consistency and editorial standards.
